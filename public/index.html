◆ Our mission

Why we're building this

Many students begin their tech journey without direction, guidance, or access to the right network. Talent is everywhere — opportunity, often, is not.

At Girls Leading Tech, we believe mentorship can change that. This cohort is designed to connect experienced women in tech with ambitious learners looking for clarity, consistency, and community.

"You can't be what you can't see. We're building a room where every girl can see herself in the people she's learning from."

◆

What mentees will learn

  • 1DSA fundamentals: arrays, strings, recursion, trees, graphs, DP
  • 2A weekly LeetCode plan + pattern-based problem solving
  • 3Resume that gets shortlisted + 1:1 review with your mentor
  • 4Mock interviews — DSA rounds, HR rounds, behavioral
  • 5How to actually apply to internships and stand out
✦

What mentors will do

  • 1Lead weekly group sessions with their cohort
  • 2Share roadmaps, resources and personal playbooks
  • 3Review work and give honest, kind feedback
  • 4Show up — consistently — for 4 weeks

● Cohort timeline

Four weeks. One playbook.

  1. 01
    Week 1

    Roadmaps, DSA Basics & Career Direction

    Find your lane, understand the landscape, and lock in a 4-week plan.

  2. 02
    Week 2

    Resume, Projects & Internship Preparation

    Build a portfolio that stands out. Sharpen the artifacts recruiters actually read.

  3. 03
    Week 3

    Mock Interviews, Networking & Growth

    Practice under pressure. Learn how to talk to people who can hire you.

  4. 04
    Week 4

    Personalized Action Plan & Final Guidance

    Walk out with a written plan for the next 90 days and a network to lean on.
